Metabolic Tracking Phases

Your local application progress ring tracks biochemical transitions dynamically based on elapsed time milestones:

04 Hours
Early Fasting

Your digestion wraps up, blood sugar stabilizes, and your insulin levels begin to drop.

12 Hours
Fat Burning

Your body runs out of stored sugar reserves and flips the metabolic switch to burn stored fat for energy.

18 Hours
Ketosis & Clarity

Your liver produces ketones to fuel your brain and body. This is the peak zone for mental focus and accelerated fat loss.

24 Hours
Cellular Cleanup

Autophagy kicks in. Your cells start recycling old, damaged parts to regenerate and naturally detoxify your body.

24+ Hours (Extended Mode)
Peak Autophagy

Your cellular cleanup hits its peak stride. Your body aggressively breaks down and recycles old, damaged cell proteins.

30 Hours
Growth Hormone Spike

Human Growth Hormone (HGH) levels skyrocket. This helps protect your lean muscle mass and accelerates tissue repair.

36 Hours
Deep Cleanse

Autophagy levels drop as the cell recycling process clears out the majority of old components, leaving behind optimized, younger-acting cells.

48 Hours
Immune Reset

Your body begins recycling old, worn-out immune cells and triggers the production of brand-new white blood cells, effectively rebooting your immune system.

Official Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the data privacy practices of the NoBS Fasting mobile application developed and published by daigof (Simple Apps / NoBS Apps) for the Android operating system.

1. Data Collection & Transmission Statement

NoBS Fasting operates under a strict Zero-Telemetry, local-first architecture. The application does not collect, capture, monitor, intercept, or transmit any personal, sensitive, or device-identifiable information to external cloud servers, databases, or third parties.

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporting tracking modules, or identity management tools. No network requests are initialized by this application during standard operation.

2. Local Storage Sandbox

All operational database logs, fasting timer counters, current streaks, milestone tracking data, and configuration metrics are stored exclusively and securely inside the application's private SQL database sandbox on your local physical device hardware storage via Room DB. Your data remains fully under your personal custody and control at all times.

3. Device Permissions Usage

  • Local System Alarms / Notifications: The app utilizes the local system Notification Manager API solely to post time-critical background push notifications when your current active fast achieves your target metabolic milestones (e.g., 4h, 12h, 18h, 24h). No tracking analytics are embedded within these notifications.

4. Children's Privacy

Because NoBS Fasting does not collect, capture, or transmit any digital information from any user device whatsoever, the application fully complies with global child privacy regulations, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

5. Policy Contact & Support Hub

For technical inquiries, feature proposals, or platform feedback, users can interact transparently with our open public communication queue on GitHub: https://github.com/daigof/nobs-fasting-feedback

Metabolic Tracking Phases

Your local application progress ring tracks biochemical transitions dynamically based on elapsed time milestones:

04 Hours
Early Fasting

Your digestion wraps up, blood sugar stabilizes, and your insulin levels begin to drop.

12 Hours
Fat Burning

Your body runs out of stored sugar reserves and flips the metabolic switch to burn stored fat for energy.

18 Hours
Ketosis & Clarity

Your liver produces ketones to fuel your brain and body. This is the peak zone for mental focus and accelerated fat loss.

24 Hours
Cellular Cleanup

Autophagy kicks in. Your cells start recycling old, damaged parts to regenerate and naturally detoxify your body.

24+ Hours (Extended Mode)
Peak Autophagy

Your cellular cleanup hits its peak stride. Your body aggressively breaks down and recycles old, damaged cell proteins.

30 Hours
Growth Hormone Spike

Human Growth Hormone (HGH) levels skyrocket. This helps protect your lean muscle mass and accelerates tissue repair.

36 Hours
Deep Cleanse

Autophagy levels drop as the cell recycling process clears out the majority of old components, leaving behind optimized, younger-acting cells.

48 Hours
Immune Reset

Your body begins recycling old, worn-out immune cells and triggers the production of brand-new white blood cells, effectively rebooting your immune system.

Official Privacy Policy

We have nothing to track because we store nothing on servers.

NoBS Fasting does not include tracking SDKs, analytics monitors, or crash reporting telemetry. All parameters, log metrics, active timers, star counts, and layout settings are saved exclusively inside the local SQL file sandbox on your hardware. No network requests are initialized by this application.

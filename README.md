Welcome to the central feedback and coordination hub for **NoBS Apps**. This repository hosts our public developer manifesto, our landing pages, and serves as an open space for our community to suggest new app concepts.

## 💡 Got an Idea for a New App?

We build tools based on human necessity. If there is a daily utility app you use that has been ruined by corporate bloat, forced cloud registrations, heavy animations, or aggressive telemetry tracking, tell us about it.

1. Head over to our [Issues Tab](https://github.com/daigof/nobs-apps/issues).
2. Click **New Issue**.
3. Detail the core utility you need and what specific "bloat" you want eliminated.
